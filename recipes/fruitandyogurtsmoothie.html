Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie

Description

This fruit and yogurt smoothie will make your day on mornings that feel like you'd rather stay in bed than attend your morning classes!
It is refreshing and tastes amazing.
Note:
The portions are for a single serving so keep this in mind :)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

    Combine all ingredients in a blender; blend until smooth.
