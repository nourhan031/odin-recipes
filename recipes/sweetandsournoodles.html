Sweet and Sour Noodles

Description

This sweet and sour noodles is my go to when i don't feel like putting too much effort into making a meal
It is nutritious and tastes so good.

Ingredients

For the Noodles:

  • Thin spaghetti
  • Bell pepper
  • snap peas
  • Baby corn
  • Sliced water chestnuts
  • Green onions
  • cilantro
  • seeds

For the Sauce:

  • vinegar
  • Honey
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Lime juice
  • Chili garlic sauce
  • Ground ginger
Note:
you can add any protein of your choice :)

Steps

  1. Cook the thin spaghetti noodles until it's done, then drain and set aside.
  2. Whisk together the sweet and sour sauce ingredients and keep near the stove for quick access later.
  3. Then, sauté sliced bell pepper in a little sesame oil until crisp-tender.
  4. Add the sugar snap peas, water chestnuts, and green onion to the same skillet and continue cooking until the veggies become slightly tender.
  5. Finally, add the cooked spaghetti and the homemade sweet and sour sauce to the skillet and toss to coat the noodles fully.
  6. Cook the Asian noodles for a few more minutes to thicken the sauce and heat
    everything through. I like to garnish mine with cilantro, sesame seeds, and more
    green onion, but you can keep things as simple as you’d like.
